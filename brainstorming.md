## New App Ideas - Evaluate and Categorize

1. Pet Finder App
   - **Category:** Lifestyle / Pet Adoption
   - **Mobile:** A mobile-first experience that utilizes geolocation, search filters, and user profiles to efficiently match potential adopters with pets in their area.
   - **Story:** Helps users find the perfect pet for their family and lifestyle by connecting them with local shelters and rescue organizations, reducing the number of pets in shelters and promoting responsible pet adoption.
   - **Market:** Potential pet adopters, shelters, and rescue organizations looking to find loving homes for their animals.
   - **Habit:** Users can browse available pets at their leisure, save their favorites, and set up alerts for new pets matching their criteria. The app becomes an integral part of the pet adoption process.
   - **Scope:** The initial version would include basic search and filtering functionality, as well as the ability to view pet profiles and contact shelters. Future versions could incorporate user profiles, in-app messaging, and additional features to support the pet adoption process.

2. Personal Finance Coach
   - **Category:** Finance / Personal Finance
   - **Mobile:** A mobile-first experience with features like expense tracking, budgeting, and personalized recommendations to help users manage their finances more effectively.
   - **Story:** Empowers users to take control of their financial health by providing personalized insights, recommendations, and coaching based on their unique financial situation.
   - **Market:** Individuals looking to improve their financial literacy, create budgets, and achieve financial goals.
   - **Habit:** Users can regularly input their expenses, check their budgets, and receive notifications about their financial progress. The app becomes a regular part of their financial management routine.
   - **Scope:** The initial version would include basic expense tracking, budgeting, and personalized insights. Future versions could incorporate features like bill reminders, investment tracking, and integration with financial institutions.

3. Local Event Discovery
   - **Category:** Entertainment / Local Events
   - **Mobile:** A mobile-first experience utilizing geolocation to help users discover nearby events, including concerts, festivals, and art shows, based on their interests and preferences.
   - **Story:** Helps users explore their local community and discover new experiences by connecting them with events tailored to their interests.
   - **Market:** Anyone looking to find local events and experiences, including locals and tourists.
   - **Habit:** Users can regularly browse events, save their favorites, and receive notifications about upcoming events matching their interests. The app becomes a go-to resource for local entertainment.
   - **Scope:** The initial version would include event discovery, personalized recommendations, and the ability to save and share events. Future versions could incorporate ticket purchasing, event check-ins, and social features for connecting with other attendees.
